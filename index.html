How AscendSAT works

We keep things simple and focused. No generic curriculum, no giant group classes — just targeted 1:1 coaching designed around how you think and where you can gain points the fastest.

1
Free consult & goal setting
We hop on a quick 20-minute call with the student (and parent if they want), talk through current scores, test date, and target goal, and answer questions.
2
Diagnostic & custom plan
In the first full session, we work through a short set of problems to identify strengths and weaknesses. You’ll get a plan of attack by section and topic.
3
Weekly 1:1 sessions + homework
Sessions focus on strategy and explanation, not just getting the answer. You’ll also receive carefully selected homework sets with easy, medium, and hard problems — plus an answer key and breakdown.
4
Measure progress and adjust
We track your scores on practice sets and official tests, then adjust the focus as you improve. The goal is steady, predictable score gains — not last-minute cramming.

Results & philosophy

The goal of AscendSAT is not just a higher score — it’s confidence under time pressure and a clear understanding of how the test “thinks.” The best score gains come from fixing your most frequent mistake patterns, not doing thousands of random questions.

We combine official-style questions, targeted drills, and pattern recognition so you learn to predict trap answers, manage time, and stay calm when a question looks unfamiliar.

FAQ

Who is AscendSAT for?
AscendSAT is best for students aiming anywhere from 1100 to 1500+ who want a strategy-based, 1:1 approach instead of a giant class. We work especially well with students who already care about improving and are willing to do short, focused homework between sessions.
How are sessions held?
All sessions are online via Zoom or Google Meet with screen sharing and a shared whiteboard. Students can join from a laptop or tablet with stable internet and a quiet space.
Do you offer just Math or just Reading/Writing?
Yes. Some students choose Math-only or Reading/Writing-only plans, while others do a mix. We’ll decide the best split based on your goals and current strengths.
How do we pay?
Payments are made directly to me via Cash App or Zelle. Once you request a package, I’ll reply with the exact handle/info to use and we’ll confirm your session times.
